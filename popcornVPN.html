Features

Secure

Military-grade encryption keeps your traffic safe from prying eyes.

Open-source

You can view and contribute to our code at any time, free of charge, so you know you can trust us.

Invisible

Scrambled headers ensure that your traffic's destination and origin remain unknown

Instance vetting

All VPN hosts are carefully vetted and tested to ensure they can meet our high standards of privacy and security.

Stable

Built on the trusted and stable OpenVPN backend to ensure that our service never fails.

Accountable

We wouldn't give you our products if we didn't trust them. hackSugar regularly vets and tests popcornVPN.
